加油的英文怎么说？真的不是fighting！

  • "John, can you please help me to print some materials? Just one minute." "Sorry, I wish I could, but I'm too occupied now."

  • "Fighting" is a word coined(created) by the Koreans, not original Enligsh. You can use "working hard" to replace it. And if you want to encourage someone, it's better to say "Go for it" .
